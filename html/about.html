About Me

This is Kyle.

A Summary

As a 26 year-old international student – a Filipino raised in Singapore, who just returned from a mission in Taiwan after serving three years as an officer in the Singapore Army, I took the road less travelled.

Fortune had it that I would live in and experience many countries and cultures to this point - serving a mission in northern Taiwan, an internship in central Germany, being raised in Singapore, and born in the capital of the Philippines, before deciding on the United States for my university education.

In the midst of all this, I see that the hand of God was truly present in each moment of my journey. I am excited to see where His brushstrokes continue to take me.

My Interests


Travel

I am truly blessed to have had opportunities to expand my worldview and appreciation of life. Many of them came as a result of pioneers before me, blazing the trail of promoting international exposure among students in the tiny island of Singapore.


Military Service

After serving for three years as a Lieutenant in the Singapore Army, I can safely say that anything to do with the military fascinates me. Time in the Army was a period of growth in every sense – physical, emotional, and spiritual. I learned that nothing is given, only earned, and that there is no greater joy than knowing you have a buddy out there watching your back. Or One who is watching from above.


Snowsports

This one is a recent find. Practically an impulsive decision given the unique experience of having our college along the Wasatch Mountain Range, I decided to enroll in a Snowboarding class this semsester and am positive this will be a lifelong hobby. It encompasses all I love into one activity - being out in nature, the rush of adrenaline given the freedom to glide to any point you desire, the sense of accomplishment from seeing consistent improvement after each iteration. The only downside would be the price which, as a broke college student, can be immense. Otherwise, I can confidently declare I am in love with the sport.
